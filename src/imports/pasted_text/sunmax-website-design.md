🎨 VISUAL STYLE & DESIGN SYSTEM
============================

Color Palette (from Figma reference — SUNMAX Solar Panel Website):
- Primary background: Deep dark navy / charcoal black (#0A0E1A or #0D1117)
- Accent primary: Vivid electric orange (#FF6B00 or #FF7A1A)
- Accent secondary: Warm golden yellow (#FFB800)
- Text primary: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Text secondary: Light gray (#A0ADB8)
- Card backgrounds: Dark glass / semi-transparent (#141B2D with 80% opacity)
- Surface elevated: Slightly lighter dark (#1A2236)
- Border/divider: Subtle blue-gray (#243050)
- Success/green accent: Electric lime (#00E676) — for stats and highlights
- Gradient hero: From #0A0E1A via #0D1A2E to #1A0A00 with orange glow

Typography:
- Headings: "Inter" or "Syne" — Bold 700/800 weight, large scale (64px H1, 48px H2, 36px H3)
- Body: "Inter" Regular 400 — 16px/24px line height
- Labels/caps: Letter-spacing 0.15em, uppercase, 12px
- Hero headline: Massive 80-96px, white with orange gradient highlight on key word

Design language:
- Dark glassmorphism cards with subtle orange glow borders
- Floating gradient orbs in background (orange + deep blue)
- Clean grid layouts with generous whitespace
- Micro-interactions implied through hover state designs
- Sharp geometric shapes mixed with soft gradients
- Technical/professional but visually stunning
- Solar panel texture patterns used as decorative elements
- Data visualization elements (stats, counters, charts)
